Hi! I'm Mark.

I started my professional career as an Economist for the U.S. Government. I left civil service return to university to pursue an advanced degree in computational physics. During my time in grad school, I also become an entrepreneur and started an independent game studio called Gigaloth. My first product as a self-made software engineer was the "National Games Winner" at Microsoft's U.S. Imagine Cup.

Eventually, my interest turned to web development as a more practical way to earn a living doing what I loved, coding. I have obtained my front end developer certification through freeCodeCamp and continue to pursue the full stack certification. My long term goals are to combine expertise in data/statistics with coding and enter the field of AI and Data Science.

Thanks for reading. Please contact me with any questions and never stop learning!
